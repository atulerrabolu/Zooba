Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a more efficient and effective way of managing and choosing courses in highschool. We want to allow students like you to be able to stay on top of your schoolwork with ease and carve a highschool experience tailormade for you. My cofounder and I were just as frustrated with how hard it is to navigate highschool easily.

When my cofounder and I gained the technical skills needed to build tools to fix these problems, we got right on it. We don't want kids to go through what we had to, and we hope that you love our product. We help streamline ways to communicate your academic standing, help you get on top of school work, gain new friendships, and strengthen the ones you already have.

Services Offered

Automatic GPA Calculation

We created an algorithm to parse the grades stored in Home Access to provide you with your most up to date GPA in a fraction of a second. All it takes is one click and a dream!

Course Reccomendation

Using our course reccommendation algorithm, we give you a list of classes you are most likely to be interested in based off of your favorite classes. We tailormake your highschool experience.

Assignment Notifier

By using a similar algorithm to that of the GPA Calculation one, we can give you the list of upcoming assignments that you can get ahead on. Site statistics show users improve their grades after using it.

Friendship

Using our user similarity algorithm, you can learn more about your friend's interests! We also recommend new friends with similar tastes as you. Laslty, you can personalize profiles and see classes others are taking.
